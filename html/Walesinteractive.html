WALES INTERACTIVE

Wales Interactive is a dynamic video game development studio based in Wales, known for creating engaging and immersive gaming experiences. Founded in 2012, they specialize in developing interactive narrative games that blend storytelling with player choices, allowing players to shape the outcome of the game.

Their portfolio includes a variety of titles, such as "The Bunker," a psychological horror game, and "Soulbound," which emphasizes narrative-driven gameplay. Wales Interactive is also recognized for producing games that often incorporate unique art styles and innovative gameplay mechanics, making them stand out in the industry.

In addition to game development, Wales Interactive is committed to promoting Welsh culture and talent through their projects. They often collaborate with local artists and writers, ensuring that their games reflect the rich heritage and creativity of Wales. Overall, Wales Interactive continues to push the boundaries of interactive storytelling, making a significant impact in the gaming world.

Wales has recently launched an interactive game centered around the ancient Sker ritual, which is deeply rooted in local folklore and traditions. This latest game invites players to explore the mystical aspects of the Sker area, known for its rich history and stunning coastal scenery.

In the game, participants embark on a journey through the landscape, uncovering secrets and engaging in challenges that reflect the Sker ritual's themes of connection to nature and community. Players can interact with various characters inspired by local legends, solve puzzles, and complete tasks that honor the traditions of the region.

The interactive format allows players to experience the Sker ritual in a modern context, blending storytelling with gameplay. As they progress, they learn about the cultural significance of the ritual, making it not just a game but an educational experience that celebrates Welsh heritage. This innovative approach has captured the interest of both locals and tourists, making it a popular attraction in Wales.
